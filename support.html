The Summer Internship Programme is designed to enhance students’ intellectual growth and practical understanding by offering firsthand exposure to real-world organizational and societal environments. Conducted after the first year of study, this internship serves as a vital bridge between academic learning and industry practices. Through direct corporate and industrial engagement, students gain invaluable insights into workplace dynamics, decision-making processes, and operational challenges. This experience not only helps them apply management theories in practical settings but also develops their problem-solving abilities, professional skills, and confidence—preparing them for future leadership roles in the business world.
